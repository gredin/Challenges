Regex Golf
==========

These are my answers to the Regex Golf challenge (http://regex.alf.nu/).

1. Plain strings
----------------

    foo

2. Anchors
----------

    k$

3. Ranges
---------

    ^[a-f]+$

4. Backrefs
-----------

    (...).*\1

5. Abba
-------

    ^((?!(.)(.)\3\2).)*$

6. A man, a plan
----------------

    ^(.)(.).*\2\1$

7. Prime
--------

    ^((?!^((xx){2,}|(xxx){2,}|(x{5}){2,})$).)*$

8. Four
-------

    (.)(.\1){3}

9. Order
--------

    ^[^o].{4,5}$

10. Triples
-----------

    (((000)|(003)|(06)|(09)|(12)|(015)|(60))$)|(^((95)|(90)|(87)|(81)|(71)|(74)|(44)|(40)|(39)|(14)|(173)|(31)|(32)|(36)))

11. Glob
--------

    ^((\*((er)|(fa)|i|p|t|v))|b|w|p|l|(re)|(ch)|(co)|(cr)|(do)|(mi))

12. Balance
-----------

    ^<([<>][<>])+>$|^$

13. Powers
----------

    ^(x|xx|x{4}|x{8}|x{16}|(x{32})|(x{64})+)$
